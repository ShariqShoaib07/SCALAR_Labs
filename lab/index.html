SCALAR Labs

Control, planning, safety, and learning for robotic systems in real-world environments

Lab Introduction

SCALAR Labs focuses on safety-compliant control, motion planning, and learning for robotics systems with parametric and environment uncertainty. Our work spans single-robot and multi-robot systems, with validation in both simulation and hardware.

Research Areas

Motion Planning Model Predictive Control Safety-Critical Control Multi-Agent Robotics LLM Task Planning

Team Members

Current Projects

  • Semi-parametric control and reinforcement learning for robust bipedal locomotion on uneven terrain.
  • Control and safety mechanisms for multi-arm robotic manipulators in human-robot interaction.
  • LLM-based task planning integrated with motion planning and control architectures.
